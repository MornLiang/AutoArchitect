A single-storey rectangular school teaching building, ground floor only.

Overall dimensions and orientation
- Footprint: 50000 mm (east-west, X) by 25000 mm (north-south, Y).
- The main entrance is on the south facade (negative-Y side); north is up in
  the reference floor plan.
- Storey height: 3600 mm. Brick load-bearing walls 200 mm thick, concrete
  slab and concrete flat roof.

Spatial program — central corridor layout
- A horizontal central corridor runs the full length of the building in the
  east-west direction. It is about 3000 mm wide and separates the two rows
  of rooms.
- North row (5 cells, left to right): stairwell-W, classroom-1, classroom-2,
  classroom-3, classroom-4, classroom-5, stairwell-E.
  (Yes — the row has 7 cells: 5 classrooms in the middle plus a staircase at
  each end.)
- South row (left to right): classroom-6, classroom-7, mens-toilet,
  lobby, womens-toilet, classroom-8, classroom-9, classroom-10.

Room sizes (approximate, the architect may adjust)
- Each classroom is roughly 7500 mm wide by 8500 mm deep, enough to fit
  desks for ~40 students.
- Each stairwell is roughly 3500 mm by 8500 mm.
- The lobby is roughly 6000 mm wide by 8500 mm deep and sits at the center
  of the south facade, aligned with the main entrance.
- The mens and womens toilets flank the lobby; each is roughly 4500 mm by
  8500 mm.

Doors
- Every classroom has one single-leaf 1000 mm wide door opening into the
  central corridor.
- Each stairwell has one door into the corridor.
- The mens and womens toilets each have one 900 mm door opening into the
  lobby (or directly into the corridor near the lobby).
- The lobby has a 1800 mm double-leaf main entrance door on the south
  facade.
- Total interior+exterior doors: about 14.

Windows
- Every classroom has two large 1800 x 1800 mm windows on its outer
  facade (north for the upper row, south for the lower row).
- The corridor has a horizontal band of windows on its short ends and
  above the lobby entrance.
- Toilets have small high-sill 900 x 600 mm windows on the south facade.
- Total windows: about 22.

Other
- No interior columns (load-bearing brick walls only).
- One flat roof slab covering the whole footprint, 200 mm thick.
- One ground-floor slab.
- Railings appear only inside the two stairwells (count as 2).
